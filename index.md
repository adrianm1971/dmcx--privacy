# Privacy Policy for DMCX

Effective date: [insert date]

DMCX is operated by Adrian Foley ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information DMCX handles, how it is used, and the choices available to users of the app.

By using DMCX, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Handle

DMCX is a property inspection and reporting app. Depending on how you use the app, DMCX may handle the following information:

### Information entered by you
- Property addresses
- Landlord, tenant, occupant, or client details
- Inspection dates
- Weather conditions
- Survey notes
- Report comments and findings

### Photos and images
- Photos taken within the app
- Photos selected from your device for use in reports

### AI Review inputs
If you choose to use AI-powered features, DMCX may process:
- Selected report text
- Selected images chosen by you for AI analysis

### Subscription information
DMCX may receive limited subscription and entitlement information from Apple in connection with in-app purchases and trial access.

## 2. How Information Is Stored

Inspection reports, entered text, and photos are stored locally on your device.

Local backup files created by the app are also stored on your device.

DMCX does not currently provide a general cloud account storage system for report data.

If you export a report, the exported file may be stored or shared using the location, app, or method you choose.

## 3. AI Review and External Processing

AI Review features are optional.

When you explicitly choose to run an AI review, selected text and selected photos are sent to the configured backend service for processing. The backend may then transmit the necessary content to an AI provider in order to generate the requested response.

AI analysis does not run automatically. Your content is only submitted when you choose to use the feature.

## 4. How Information Is Used

Information handled by DMCX is used to:
- Create, edit, and manage inspection reports
- Store photos and report content locally
- Generate PDF and document exports
- Provide AI-assisted analysis when requested
- Create and restore local backups
- Manage subscriptions, trials, and entitlement access
- Improve app performance and reliability

## 5. Legal Basis and Purpose

Where applicable, information is processed only as necessary to provide the app’s requested functionality, including reporting tools, exports, subscriptions, and optional AI review features initiated by the user.

## 6. Sharing of Information

DMCX does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only where necessary:
- With backend and AI processing providers when you request AI Review
- With Apple for subscription, billing, and entitlement services
- Where required by law or legal process

Only the minimum necessary information is shared for these purposes.

## 7. Data Retention

Reports, photos, and local backup files remain on your device until deleted by you.

If AI Review content is submitted, temporary retention may depend on the backend provider or AI service configuration in use at that time.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to help protect information processed through the app and associated backend services. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 9. Your Choices

You may:
- Use the free version of the app without subscription
- Use AI Review during the trial period, after which AI features require an active subscription
- Choose whether to use AI Review features
- Delete reports, backups, and photos stored locally
- Manage camera, photo library, microphone, and speech recognition permissions through your device settings
- Contact us with privacy-related questions
- Request deletion-related assistance by contacting us at adrianfoley1971@icloud.com

## 10. Children’s Privacy

DMCX is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

## 11. International Processing

If AI Review features are used, submitted content may be processed in countries outside your own depending on the service providers used.

## 12. Changes to This Policy
